Biography

Thank you very much for visiting my personal homepage, this is a great privilege and honor for me !

Z. Zhang is currently a Ph.D Candidate (Informatics) with Osaka University, Suita, Japan. He received B.A. degree in Japanese from Changzhou Institute of Technology, Changzhou, China, in 2013 and the M.Sc. degree in Mathematics from Guilin University of Electronic Technology, Guilin, China, in 2020. From 2019 to 2020, he has been a Visiting Master Student at EECS, The University of Kitakyushu, Fukuoka, Japan. His research interests include sparse modeling, robust optimization, and control theory.
He is a student member of SIAM, INFORMS, and IEEE.

The Story of My Name

My surname is ‘‘Zhang’’, which is a family name passed down from my (grand)father and ancestors.
My first name, ‘‘Zhicheng’’, is literally derived from Chinese interpretation of the proverb ‘‘Where there's a will, there's a way’’.
Such a given name signifies my family's hope for me to become an honest, kind, and persistent person.
Consequently, both my parents and friends usually call me Charlie.
